Help & Support

Vendor Help Center

Common questions about running your shop and getting paid.

Premium vendors get the ⭐ VIP Badge, the Golden Glow, and appear at the very top of their category. You also unlock Unlimited Shop Photos and Custom Niches to drive hyper-targeted traffic. Standard shops are limited to 10 photos and cannot create niches.
Click "Activate Trial". A Paystack popup will ask for your card to authorize future payments, but you are charged ₦0 today. You instantly unlock all Premium features. If you love the traffic, do nothing—₦40,000 will be auto-debited after 7 days. If you don't want it, hit "Cancel Subscription" before day 7.
Standard shops are capped at 10 Shelf Items. Once you hit 10 photos, the upload form is replaced with an upgrade prompt. To unlock unlimited uploads and show more of your stock, upgrade to Premium!
When a buyer clicks "Message Shop" on your profile, it opens a direct WhatsApp chat with your number. You handle the negotiation, the deal, and the payment directly. We don't touch your money or take commissions.
Custom Niches are hyper-specific tags (like "UK Used iPhones" or "Vintage Denim") that Premium vendors create. These show up on the main Discover page for all buyers to see, driving highly targeted traffic straight to your shop instead of generic category pages.
If you are currently a Premium vendor, you will see a "Cancel Subscription" button at the top of this dashboard. Clicking it will immediately revoke your VIP status and stop any future auto-debits from Paystack. Your profile will revert to Standard.
The Homepage "Spotlight" is reserved exclusively for VIP/Premium vendors. Standard vendors are listed inside their specific category pages. To get on the homepage and get maximum visibility, you need to activate your Premium trial or upgrade.
