Finding Vendors

Use the Smart Search bar at the top of the homepage to search by name, category (e.g., "Fashion"), or location (e.g., "Lagos"). Alternatively, click "Discover" to browse all categories and specific niches.
Custom Niches are hyper-specific tags created by top vendors (e.g., "UK Used iPhones" or "Vintage Denim"). If you click on a Niche box, it takes you straight to a vendor who specializes in exactly that item.
Vendors with the ⭐ VIP badge are Premium Vendors. They are highly active, have unlimited inventory, and appear at the very top of search results and categories. They are committed to providing the best service on the platform.
Those are our VIP Vendors! They get premium placement because they have upgraded their accounts, meaning they are serious businesses with lots of stock to sell.

Safety & Issues

We curate vendors, but because transactions happen off-platform via WhatsApp, we cannot guarantee them. We recommend using trusted payment methods, asking for proof of stock, and using delivery services you trust. VIP vendors are generally the most reliable.
If a vendor scams you, please use the contact form below immediately. Select "Vendor Complaint / Fraud". We take this very seriously and will ban them from the platform immediately to protect other buyers.
